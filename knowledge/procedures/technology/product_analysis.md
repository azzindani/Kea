# Procedure: Product Analysis

Step-by-step process for evaluating and comparing technology-based products or services.

### Steps
1. **Category Definition**: Define the precise product category and its primary use cases.
2. **Feature Mapping**: Construct a feature comparison matrix against direct competitors.
3. **Pricing Audit**: Analyze pricing models (SaaS, tier-based, consumption-based).
4. **Integration Scan**: Assess the breadth and quality of available APIs and integration capabilities.
5. **Vendor Health**: Evaluate the stability of the vendor, their funding history, and their public roadmap.
6. **Sentiment Collection**: Aggregate user sentiment from professional reviews, GitHub issues, and community forums.
