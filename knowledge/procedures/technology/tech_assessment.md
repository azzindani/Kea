# Procedure: Technology Assessment

Standard operating procedure for performing a high-level technology landscape evaluation.

### Steps
1. **Stack Mapping**: Map the core technology stack and its internal/external dependencies.
2. **Ecosystem Audit**: Identify key commercial and open-source players and their respective market positions.
3. **Maturity Check**: Assess technology maturity (e.g., positioning on the Gartner Hype Cycle).
4. **License Review**: Evaluate the balance between open-source and proprietary components in the domain.
5. **IP Analysis**: Analyze patent activity trends and R&D investment levels across the industry.
6. **Adoption Profiling**: Assess adoption curves, developer sentiment, and the health of the associated ecosystem.
7. **Disruption Scan**: Identify high-potential emerging technologies that could disrupt the current status quo.
