# Procedure: Competitive Analysis

Universal framework for mapping and evaluating the competitive positioning of players within a specific market.

### Steps
1. **Boundary Mapping**: Define the industry and geographical boundaries of the market.
2. **Player Identification**: Map the top 10-20 major players and emerging challengers.
3. **Share Audit**: Analyze the distribution of market share across the identified players.
4. **Barrier Assessment**: Evaluate operational, legal, and economic barriers to entry.
5. **Differentiation Review**: Analyze the unique value propositions and differentiation strategies of each firm.
6. **SWOT Scan**: Identify emerging threats and strategic opportunities within the landscape.
7. **Matrix Production**: Generate a comparative matrix summarizing the competitive status quo.
