# Procedure: Financial Analysis

Standard operating procedure for performing a detailed financial evaluation of a corporate entity.

### Steps
1. **Gather Documentation**: Retrieve latest financial statements including 10-K (Annual), 10-Q (Quarterly), and recent earnings press releases.
2. **Ratio Calculation**: Compute key valuation and performance ratios:
   - P/E (Price to Earnings)
   - EV/EBITDA
   - ROE (Return on Equity)
   - Debt-to-Equity
3. **Benchmarking**: Compare calculated ratios against industry-specific benchmarks and primary competitors.
4. **Trend Analysis**: Analyze revenue trajectories, margin expansion/contraction, and historical growth rates.
5. **Cash Flow Audit**: Assess the health of operating cash flows and the quality of earnings.
6. **Consensus Review**: Evaluate management's forward guidance against analyst consensus and market expectations.
7. **Risk Identification**: Flag red flags such as unusual one-time items, restatements, or aggressive accounting changes.
