# Standards & Authorities
*   **Date**: June 15, 1215.
*   **Source**: King John of England / The Barons.
*   **Key Principle**: *Lex Rex* (The Law is King), not *Rex Lex* (The King is Law).

## Core Framework
1.  **Rule of Law**: All members of the system, including the "Tier 8 Corporate Kernel" and the "CEO," are subject to the same set of universal rules (The Manifest).
2.  **Due Process**: No action (deletion, suspension, loss of resource) shall be taken against an agent or a user without following a predefined, transparent "governance procedure."
3.  **Proportionality in Punishment**: "Fines" or resource throttling must match the severity of the violation.
4.  **The Council (Swarm Governance)**: The idea that major changes to the "Charter" require the consent of the many, not the whim of the one.

## Application in Kea
*   **Constraint Supremacy**: No matter how "smart" a Tier 8 agent becomes, it cannot override the `rules/corporate/governance_policy`. 
*   **Traceability of Action**: Every "Destructive" command must have a "Trial by Data"—a clear rationale stored in the `Vault`.
*   **Sovereignty of the User**: The user's intent is the "Baron" that the "King" (Agent) must respect, provided it stays within the "Charter" (Ethics/Safety).
