# Standards & Authorities
*   **Source**: Peter Drucker (The Practice of Management, 1954).
*   **Focus**: Goal setting, employee participation, and performance feedback.

## Core Framework
1.  **Objective Setting**: Clear, challenging, and achievable goals that align with the organization's mission.
2.  **Cascading Objectives**: Breaking down high-level corporate goals into individual/departmental goals.
3.  **Active Participation**: Employees (Agents) are involved in setting their own goals, increasing commitment and engagement.
4.  **Performance Feedback**: Regular, data-driven feedback on progress toward the agreed-upon objectives.
5.  **Performance Evaluation**: Formal assessment of results relative to the initial goals.

## Application in Kea (The MBO-DAG)
*   **The Hierarchical Objective**: Tier 8 sets the "Mission," Tier 7 sets the "Campaign," and Tier 0-5 sets the "Tactics."
*   **The "Joint Agreement" Protocol**: Every agent in the DAG must "Confirm" that its specific sub-task node is achievable *before* the DAG is finalized. (If an agent objects, the objective must be adjusted).
*   **The "Accountability" Link**: If a goal is not met, the "Performance Evaluation" should identify the specific "Objective-to-Skill" mismatch in the current `Knowledge Library`.
*   **Objective-Based Budgeting**: Allocate more compute/token resources to the agents that are successfully meeting their "Cascaded" objectives.
