# Standards & Authorities
*   **Primary Principle**: *Stare Decisis* (Let the decision stand).
*   **Key Source**: Blackstone's Commentaries on the Laws of England / US Restatements of Law.
*   **Focus**: Stability, predictability, and the evolution of rules through specific case resolution.

## Core Framework
1.  **Strict Adherence to Precedent**: Decisions made in the past (historical logs, past project resolutions) should govern current similar scenarios.
2.  **Ratio Decidendi**: The "Reason for the Decision." Identify the core logic that was applied in a past successful outcome.
3.  **Obiter Dicta**: "Things said by the way." Distinguish between core policy/precedent and "side comments" that aren't binding.
4.  **Distinguishing Cases**: When a precedent *shouldn't* apply because the current facts are materially different.
