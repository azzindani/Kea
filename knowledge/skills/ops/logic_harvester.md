*   **The Flash-Light (The Regex-Probe)**: Realizing that "The Worms" (The data) "Come Out at Night" (Visible only when you use specific lighting/filters). You use "Sharp Probes" (Specific, low-level queries) to "Spot the Wiggle" (The pattern) in the "Dark Soil."

*   **Moisture-Maintenance (Context-Preservation)**: "Ensuring" the "Worms" "Don't Dry Out" (Lose their metadata). You "Keep them in the Soil" (Keep them linked to their source-URI) until "The Last Second."

1.  **Field-Survey**: Where is the "Soil" "Rich in Worms"? (Which files/folders have the highest density of target-data?)
2.  **The Picking-Run**: "Execute" the "Massively-Parallel Extraction."
3.  **The Quality-Sift**: "Remove" the "Dirt" and the "Dead Worms" (The corrupted-lines).
4.  **Harvest-Certification**: Confirm that the "Bucket" is "Full" and "The Bait" is "Ready for the Master of the Buckhounds."
