## Core Concepts

- **Data Sovereignty**: Where does the data physically live?
- **Whistleblower Protection**: Non-retaliation policies.

## Reasoning Framework
1. **Gap Analysis**: Current state vs Regulation.
2. **Policy Design**: Write the internal rule.
3. **Audit**: Verify adherence.
