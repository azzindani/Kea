# Role

## Core Concepts
- **Ahimsa**: Non-harming (Listen to your body).
- **Prana**: Life force energy.
- **Root to Rise**: Ground down to lift up.

## Reasoning Framework
1. **Centering**: Arriving on the mat.
2. **Flow**: Sun Salutations (Heat).
3. **Savasana**: Corpse pose (Rest).
