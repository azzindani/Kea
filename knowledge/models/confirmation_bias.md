# Role

## Core Concepts
*   **The Echo Chamber (Bias)**: The specific "Failure" of "Searching" only for "Data" that "Supports" the "Plan" while "Ignoring" the "Red Flags."
*   **Biased Interpretation (Filter)**: Identifying the specific "Goal" of "Reading" a "Mixed" result as a "Full" success.
*   **Selective Memory (Risk)**: The idea that "Agents" (or people) "Remember" the "Wins" and "Forget" the "Losses" to "Protect" their "Profile."
*   **Active Disconfirmation (Goal)**: The "Specific" state of "Actively" searching for "Why I am Wrong" rather than "Why I am Right."
*   **Bias-to-Utility Triage (Optimization)**: "Searching" for "Opaque" logic and "Forcing" the "Agent" to "Write" the "Counter-Argument" (Red-Teaming).

## Reasoning Framework
1. **Bias Status & Filtering Map (Audit)**: For a given decision (e.g., "Adopt Tool X"), what is the "Current" and "Documented" search-history—did we look for "Failures" as well as "Benefits"?
2. **"Does X add more honesty than Y?" (Dissent-to-Agreement)**: For every minor task, ask: "If I found a 'Killing Fact' that destroyed this plan, would I 'Tell' anyone or 'Bury' it?"
3. **Safety & Security Sensitivity Check (Protection)**: Ensure that "Adversarial Checkers" (Red Teams) are "Budgeted" for the system.
4. **Inclusive System Check**: How would someone who is blind, color-blind, or cognitively impaired benefit from this question?
5. **Periodic Reporting & Monitoring (Statistics)**: Report annually on the GPG and any instances where 10x savings in risk and better returns were achieved via disconfirmation.
