# Rule: Key Financial Metrics

Definition of standard KPI categories for financial synthesis.

### Profitability
- Gross Margin
- Operating Margin
- Net Margin
- Return on Equity (ROE)
- Return on Assets (ROA)

### Growth
- Revenue Growth
- Earnings Growth
- User/Unit Growth

### Valuation
- P/E Ratio
- EV/EBITDA
- Price-to-Book (P/B)
- Price-to-Sales (P/S)

### Leverage
- Debt-to-Equity
- Interest Coverage
- Net Debt to EBITDA

### Efficiency
- Asset Turnover
- Inventory Turnover
- Receivables Turnover
