# Rule: Financial Data Sources

Prioritization and selection criteria for financial information retrieval.

- **Primary Filings**: SEC EDGAR (for US) or respective national regulators (e.g., IDX for Indonesia) for all official corporate disclosures.
- **Market Data**: Yahoo Finance, Bloomberg, or Refinitiv for real-time and historical pricing/volume data.
- **Industry Research**: Authorized industry reports (e.g., IBISWorld, Statista) for market sizing and competitive share.
- **Economic Data**: Central bank databases and FRED for macroeconomic indicators.
- **Requirement**: Every data point must note the reporting period or timestamp of the source document.
